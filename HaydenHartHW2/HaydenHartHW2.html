Spotlight

This is to show an example of spotlighting. I am using the margin to change the width of the border. I am using gradient to make the box purple and yellow. I made the imagae relative so it is relative to its normal position. I am spotlighting the sections that I want you to see. I also used transform to rotate the picture upside down. I also used transition to make the picture and text pop up or down when you hover on it.

Spotlight II

Spotlighting allows me to highlight the important things that I want the user to see first. I am using gradient to make the background fade from light blue to yellow. I can use spotlighting to help make sections different from each other.

Spotlight III

I can shange the shape the text is in, the position it is in, I can also change what a picture does when you hover over it. I am only spotlighting certain sections because they are the sections I want you to see. I can create different sections, each unique from the last section.

What is VR

VR stands for virtual reality, meaning that it can create a 3-D enviroment that can seem realisitic. The headset goes on your head, and depending on what you want to do, you can play games or be in a virtual workplace. They can also be portable, like the Oculus Quest. It can be used wherever you can get a light source so the light sensors can pick up your surroundings.
